Academics

FBLA

In this club, students participate in hands-on events, as well as learn business and leadership skills.

ASL Club (American Sign Language)

In ASL club, students learn about the importance of sign language, and how to use it.

Literary Magazine Club

If you like writing, this is the place to go! With many different styles, such as poems and artwork, you can find fun and joy in writing, and possibly improve your English grade.

FFA (Future Farmers of America)

FFA encourages students to improve leadership skills, while also teaching them about agriculture and such.

FCCLA (Family, Career and Community Leaders of America)

FCCLA helps students develop community and leadership skills, while also doing fun food-related activities.

Robotics Club

Robotics club allows students to play around with robots, while also being able to do other STEM activities, such as the First Tech Challenge.

Mathcounts

In Mathcounts, students learn about many different parts of math, and get to participate in fun math contests.

Science Olympiad

A place to compete in science contests; from potions to lasers, different contests present different challenges to take on.
