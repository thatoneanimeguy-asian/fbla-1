Academics

Hover over or click on an image to learn about the respective club.

In FBLA, students participate in hands-on events, as well as learn business and leadership skills.
In ASL (American Sign Language) club, students learn about the importance of sign language, and how to use it.
If you like writing, literary magazine club is the place to go! With many different styles, such as poems and artwork, you can find fun and joy in writing, and possibly improve your English grade.
FFA (Future Farmers of America) encourages students to improve leadership skills, while also teaching them about agriculture and such.
FCCLA (Family, Career and Community Leaders of America) helps students develop community and leadership skills, while also doing fun food-related activities.
Robotics club allows students to play around with robots, while also being able to do other STEM activities, such as the First Tech Challenge.
In French club, students can explore the wonders of France, while participating in many fun activities.
In TSA (Technology Student Association), students learn about leadership, friendship, and community service, while also having access to 3D printers and CNC routers.
In history club, students get a chance to learn about the wonders of the world, and also can participate in the 2025 Virginia History Day Contest.
In Mathcounts, students learn about many different parts of math, and get to participate in fun math contests.
Science Olympiad. A place to compete in science contests; from potions to lasers, different contests present different challenges to take on.
